# Code of Conduct

RiskKernel is an open, welcoming project. We want participation to be a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and considerate in discussion, review, and disagreement.
- Assume good faith; critique ideas and code, not people.
- Welcome newcomers and help them get started.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language or imagery.
- Personal or political attacks; sustained disruption of discussions.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, discussions, and any
official communication channels — and when representing the project in public.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the maintainer at **adarsh.prashar32@gmail.com**.
Reports are handled confidentially and reviewed as promptly as possible.
Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions, comments, or contributors
that violate this policy.
